Supports displaying coordinates and orientation, plus fast travel.

Usage: Load each into the script.

Settings    
No specific configuration required

Usage

X, Y, Z, Yaw, and Pitch will be displayed at the bottom
Switch to a sub-weapon (like Pistol) and zoom to fast travel in the direction of your view (in 10m increments)
